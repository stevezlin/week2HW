About me

Steve Lin has been working in the education field for the past several years. He graduated from San Jose State University 2010 with a Bachelors in Communication Studies. After graduating, he worked at Stanford Univeristy in the Office of Accesible Education. While being there, he started getting interested in tech as he helped his friend's company at Buscomp on the weekends. During that time, an opportunity presented itself to teach English in China. Steve went abroad to teach English at Zhicheng Collenge in Fuzhou China. After being abroad, Steve came back to teach as a sub but his desire for learning new technologies continued to stir. Now he has decided to take the leap of faith to pursue the desire that has always been within. Please embark with him as he starts his new journey!

Journey to Class

January 28, 2019

Today was the first day of class. Coming from the South Bay, it was a bit of an adventure to get to class. I normally drive to most places so taking BART was a new experience since I only used it once previously. Since I live in San Jose, I have to drive 25 minutes to a Warm Springs (Nearest station) and its another 40 minutes ride. I actually jumped on the one going to San Francisco without realizing but I was able to ask a lady on the train and she was able to tell me where to get off and transfer.

Today in class, we got introduced to HTML. HTML is the a mark up language used to make websites. I was able to make my first website saying"Hello World" and publish it. We went over a lot in a short period of time. It was fun but a lot to learn.
